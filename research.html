Overview

On the theoretical side, I am engaged in solving technical problems related to dynamic macroeconomic models, such as

  • existence and uniqueness of fixed points in the presence of unbounded state spaces and rewards,
  • stability and ergodicity in generic macroeconomic dynamical systems, and
  • reducing the dimensionality in numerical computation.

    • On the practical side, I aim to apply the theory that we develop to the study of wealth inequality issues, such as mechanisms that generate fat-tailed wealth distributions.
