Overview

On the theoretical side, I am engaged in solving technical problems in dynamic macroeconomics, including

  • existence and uniqueness of solutions in presence of unbounded rewards,
  • stability and ergodicity of macroeconomic dynamical systems, and
  • reduction of dimensionality in numerical computation.

    • On the practical side, I aim to apply the theory we develop to study wealth inequality issues, including

  • the quantitative impact of capital income risk on wealth inequality, and
  • different mechanisms that generate fat-tailed wealth distributions.
