Qingyin Ma --- Research

Overview

On the theoretical side, my research is about establishing optimality and stochastic stability under generic macroeconomic setups, such as existence and uniqueness of fixed points and stationary distributions. On the applied side, the theory that I develop serves the research in the field of macroeconomics, such as income and wealth inequality.
