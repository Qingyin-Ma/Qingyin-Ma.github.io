About Myself

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the research school of Economics, Australian National University, where I obtained my Ph.D degree of Economics in 2018. My primary research interests include Quantitative Economics and Macroeconomic Theory. My most recent research projects are focused on wealth inequality issues, such as mechanisms that generate fat-tailed wealth distributions.


I will be available for intereviews at the ASSA meetings in Atlanta, Georgia, 4--6 January 2019.
