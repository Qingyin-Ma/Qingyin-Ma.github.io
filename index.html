About Myself

I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Research School of Economics, Australian National University. I obtained my Ph.D degree of Economics at the Australian National University in 2018.

My primary research interests include Quantitative Economics and Macroeconomic Theory. My most recent research projects are concerned with wealth inequality, with a particular focus on mechanisms that generate fat-tailed wealth distributions.


I will be available for intereviews at the ASSA 2019 meeting to be held on January 4-6, 2019 in Atlanta, Georgia, USA.
